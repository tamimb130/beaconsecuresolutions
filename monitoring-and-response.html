How to choose

Match the level to the risk.

A quick way to think about it — and you can run different levels at different sites.

  • Staffed, lower-risk site? Self-monitored alerts keep your team in the loop.
  • Unstaffed, medium risk? Professional monitoring verifies before you're bothered.
  • High-value or high-crime? Verified response deters and dispatches in real time.
The verification process

Real threat, or false alarm?

When AI flags an event, a trained operator reviews live video in seconds, confirms whether it's a genuine threat, and escalates per your plan — talk-down, dispatch, or police coordination.

  • Median verify time< 30 sec
  • Talk-downLive, two-way
  • DispatchPatrol / guard
  • ReportingPer incident
Liability & insurance

Verified, documented response reduces loss — and exposure.

Unverified alarms get ignored and undocumented incidents become disputes. Beacon Secure verifies every real event and produces an evidence-ready record — which can lower claims, support recovery, and strengthen your position after an incident.
