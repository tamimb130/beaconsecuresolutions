How it works

Detection is intelligent. Response is your choice.

A Beacon Secure deployment is four moving parts working together — engineered so you only hear about what matters, and respond exactly how you want to.

The model

Four steps, one continuous loop.

Step 1 · AI tower + sensors

Detect

Solar-powered towers carry PTZ and fixed cameras, strobe/speaker, and on-board AI. Computer vision classifies people, vehicles and plates and flags intrusion or loitering — not every shadow.

Step 2 · Your chosen level

Monitor

AI Monitoring sends smart alerts to your team; Live Monitoring puts trained operators on your site 24/7. Use either standalone, and mix and match across sites.

Step 3 · Alerts to action

Respond

From a push notification to live audio talk-down that warns intruders off in real time, plus instant escalation to your team or local authorities.

Step 4 · Footage & reports

Document

Every event is captured with time-stamped footage, incident reports and time-lapse — evidence-ready for insurance, disputes and owners.
